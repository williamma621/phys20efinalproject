Luminosity measures the Power of an object emitting light, and flux measures the density of light power another object recieves.

The further the object emitting the light, the lesser light is recieved, which means lower Flux

To calculate the Flux an object recieves, we have the equation:

Flux = Luminosity / (4 x Pi x Distance)

A Black Body is something that absorbs all energy falling on it, many stars are almost perfect black bodies

For Black bodies that emits lights, we can know the Luminosity of the light by knowing the Temperature and Radius of the Black Body

To calculate the Luminosity knowing the Temperature and Radius, we have the equation:
